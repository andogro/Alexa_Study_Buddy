A quiz module for anyone with Alexa

As you know, Alexa is a great addition to the household. There are a lot of quiz skills out there, but you can't create your own unless you know how to program, get it submitted and approved by Amazon etc.

Study Buddy lets you create your own quizzes for Alexa. It you are studying for a test, just enter in your information and you can practice with Alexa. Or create fun quizzes for your friends.
