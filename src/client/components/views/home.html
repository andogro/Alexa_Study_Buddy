Welcome to Study Buddy, a multiple choice style quiz creator for Alexa and the Amazon Echo.

To begin a quiz, say "Alexa, Launch Study Buddy". She will ask you for a quiz number, say the number of the quiz you would like to try and the questions will begin!

Study Buddy lets you create your own quizzes for Alexa. It you are studying for a test, just enter in your information and you can practice with Alexa. Or create fun quizzes for your friends.

Creating your own quiz is simple - all you need an account (and Alexa). Register or Login to get started
