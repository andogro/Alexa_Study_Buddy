A quiz creator for anyone with Alexa

As you know, Alexa is a great addition to the household. There are a lot of quiz skills out there, but you can't create your own unless you know how to program and then get your quiz submitted and approved by Amazon.

Study Buddy lets you create your own quizzes for Alexa by filling out simple forms. If you are an auditory learner, or want a different way to study besides flipping through another pile of flash cards, this is a great way to practice for a test!

You can also create fun trivia style quizzes for your friends and family. All quizzes created by the community are available to try. Give it a whirl - just say "Alexa, Study Buddy!"
